about

My name is Spencer Kuzara.

I am 26 years old and have been involved with computers and computer technology almost my entire life. When I was 8 years old I wanted a computer for my birthday - my father had to think about it.

Being the forward thinker that he is, instead of buying me a computer he bought me parts. If i wanted a computer I would have to build it myself.

At that point I began a journey that's lasted another ~20 years (and I've got long way to go!).

I've taken every networking/programming/graphic design class that was offered to me and recently I've sought to further my education in computer programming by enrolling at 42 Programming University.

This webpage is a compilation of my projects done either inside the free code camp program, or on my own.
