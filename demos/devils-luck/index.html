  • 👈 - pass money to the left

  • 🤑 - keep money
  • 💸 - put money in the pot
  • 🍻 - drink or put money in the pot
  1. Arrange at least three players in a circle
  2. Choose a starting buy-in (typically five $1 bills)
  3. Spin, then act on as many emojis as you have $1 bills, max of 3 (ex. if you only have $2, you will only do the first two actions and ignore the third)
  4. If you don’t have any money, skip your turn
  5. Even if you can’t spin, you aren’t out of the game until someone wins
  6. Play continues until there is one person with money left, and they also win the pot
